Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Trava’s reservation fee?

    Reservation fee is at Php100,000.

  • Where is the property located?

    Malitlit, Sta. Rosa, Laguna.

  • What are the products offered?

    Trava offers lot only.

  • What are the setbacks?

    Two (2) meters on sides and rear; Four (4) meters front.

  • What are the details on combined lots?

    One (1) model house to be built on the two (2) lots.

  • What are the details for electrical wiring?

    Electrical is fully underground; owners will tap to meter center located at the ends of the block. Cost of wire shall be for the account of the owner.

  • How much is the architectural and engineering design?

    Approximately Php500,000. In Solen Residences and Pramana Residential Park, we sold at Php65,000; consultants’ costs are Php32,000 (Jagnusten Model House) and Php28,000 (Casa Arkitektura Model House).

  • Would there be a royalty fee?

    Royalty is already part of the plan.

  • How much is the construction bond?

    Construction bond is approximately 3-5% of house cost per HOA; in Pramana Residential Park, it is Php250,000 (approximately 3-5% of house cost) – for management decision.

  • When is the turnover for the project?

    Per HLURB – Phase 1 – December 2022.

  • What are the security features of the project?

    Security features include:

    • Security system of the perimeter
    • RFID
    • CCTV cameras and strategic locations
    • Installed metal spikes on top of fence at perimeter fence at sides
  • How much will the association dues cost?

    Initial computation at Php10 per sqm. like Solen Residences and Pramana Residential Park.
