Santa Rosa: The Lion City is roaring its way to growth

09 July 2021

Key infrastructure projects within and outside Metro Manila will help drive economic growth beyond 2021, according to the recent Colliers Philippine Property Market Report.

Most of these new buildings are rising in middle-tier cities.

Southern Luzon is one of the fastest growing regions in the country. In Laguna alone, several infrastructure projects are expected to push industrial and residential values in the locale.

The Cavite-Laguna Expressway (CALAX), for instance, is projected to significantly ease traffic congestion and provide efficient transport facilities in key development areas such as Santa Rosa, Governor’s Drive and Tagaytay.

In the case of Santa Rosa, dubbed as the “Lion City of South Luzon,” greater access and ease of mobility are contributing to its progress and growth as a strategic residential, industrial, and recreational hub.

Today, the thriving city is home to masterplanned communities that are attracting business owners, investors and homebuyers.

Greenfield city: An inspired way of living

One such community is the 400-hectare Greenfield City, the brainchild of Greenfield Development Corp.

This township is located at the heart of Santa Rosa, Laguna.

Patterned after a park-living concept, Greenfield City puts a premium on open spaces and nature and offers a refuge for those who are looking for a refreshing shift in scenery and lifestyle outside of Metro Manila.

The concept of building offices, condominiums, supermarkets and restaurants within one community is now more important than ever as it satisfies the homebuyer’s demand for greater mobility and convenience—features only a masterplanned community could provide.

Greenfield City is a fine example, as it is strategically located just a few kilometers away from the South Luzon Expressway via the Greenfield City-Unilab interchange and the Cavite-Laguna Expressway – making it easily accessible from Metro Manila.

Retail outlets

As a business hub, Greenfield City also has retail outlets and al fresco dining which provide a wide variety of choices for shoppers and diners.

Residential establishments in Greenfield City are well within reach of landmark business, commercial and industrial centers.

For Greenfield Development Corp, creating lasting, sustainable living spaces is at the core of everything it stands for.

Its mantra, “Building for Generations,” exemplifies the company’s commitment to contribute to the progress of the country by pioneering future-proofed, masterplanned communities with environment-friendly and architecturally sound homes that can be inherited by many more generations to come.

Also part of Greenfield City are Zadia and Trava, residential developments that look toward the future. These projects incorporate sustainable concepts such as underground utilities, solar-powered green roofs, and the use of eco-friendly building materials to create a peaceful, sustainable luxurious haven of suburban living.

These and other developments are transforming Santa Rosa, Laguna from a once quiet and sleepy city to a vibrant and fast-growing economic hub.
